Fried Rice

Description

This is the best fried rice recipe you'll ever make. I would go so far to say that this is even the best fried rice recipe to exist! Nothing beats some freshly fried fried rice, that you made on your own wok in your own kitchen. Follow along for some epic food tips.

Ingredients

  • Jasmine Rice
  • Onions
  • Green Onions
  • Eggs
  • Chicken
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Seeds

Steps

  1. Cook Eggs and chicken, then remove from pan.
  2. Throw onions and green onions into pan.
  3. When onions and green onions give off a good smell add rice and proteins.
  4. Wait till rice becomes brownish then add soy sauces and sesame oil.
  5. Garnish with sesame seeds and eat.
