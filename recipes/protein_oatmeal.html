Protein Oatmeal

Description

Protein oatmeal for acquiring those gainz. If you wanna become swoll you gotta make this every morning with a couple o' eggs. Hit the gym a couple of minutes after you consume this feast. Trust you will become bigger than Arnold.

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Protein Powder
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Put half a cup of oatmeal and a cup of milk in a bowl.
  2. Microwave for 2 minutes.
  3. Put a scoop of protein powder and mix.
  4. Add butter and sugar as desired.
  5. Make some coffee and enjoy :).
